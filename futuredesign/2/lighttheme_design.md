## Brand & Style
The design system reflects a bright, sun-drenched approach to premium craftsmanship, drawing inspiration from high-end organic packaging and light-toned European or Mediterranean galleries. The personality remains authoritative, sophisticated, and deeply rooted in heritage, presenting an airy, warm, and natural atmosphere.
The visual style is **Minimalist / High-Contrast**, built on a light-mode layout that uses generous warm negative space to emphasize gold typography, fine borders, and natural product photography. The "Jamhawi" aesthetic is achieved through a combination of traditional serif elegance and modern geometric layout, establishing an editorial "lookbook" tone.
## Colors
The palette is anchored in a soft ivory and alabaster base to create a clean, luminous backdrop for rich product details and metallic gold finishes.
- **Primary (Deep Metallic Gold):** Adjusted to `#9E7B28` for improved contrast on light surfaces, used for brand-critical typography, active states, and decorative borders.
- **Secondary (Burnt Umber / Dark Earth):** A rich chocolate-brown (`#5C4636`) inspired by natural date colors and dark wood finishes, utilized for deep textural elements and clear UI separators.
- **Neutral (Alabaster & Charcoal):** The foundation of the user interface. Backgrounds use warm alabaster `#FAF8F5`, while surface elements use pure white `#FFFFFF` or soft linen `#EFECE6`.
- **Text:** Headlines utilize a deep obsidian charcoal (`#1C1B1B`) or primary gold to establish clear hierarchy, while body copy uses a soft, legible charcoal grey (`#474746`).
## Typography
Typography drives the sophisticated, literary tone of the brand. **Bodoni Moda** is selected for headlines to provide high-contrast serifs that suggest high-end editorial layouts. **Manrope** provides a clean, neutral sans-serif counterpoint for body copy to ensure comfortable reading.
Key treatments:
- **Serif Headlines:** Utilized for product titles, key editorial statements, and hero sections to ground the design.
- **Micro-copy:** Small, uppercase Manrope labels with generous letter spacing (10%) are used for category tags and packaging metadata, mimicking artisanal label stamps.
## Layout & Spacing
The desktop layout uses a **Fixed Grid** philosophy to create a structured lookbook feel. Content is centered within a 1280px container to guide the viewer’s eye.
- **Breathing Room:** Large vertical gaps (stack-lg) are used between major content blocks to create a relaxed, unhurried reading pace.
- **Asymmetry:** Asymmetrical placement of product imagery breaks the grid naturally, allowing rich lifestyle photography to flow across the page.
- **Mobile:** Transitions to a single-column layout with 24px side margins, emphasizing centered text blocks and vertical image cards.
## Elevation & Depth
Depth is created through **Tonal Layers** and **Soft Shadows** rather than high-contrast dark backdrops.
- **Surfaces:** Use a transition from soft linen (`#EFECE6`) to pure white (`#FFFFFF`) to distinguish interactive containers from the background.
- **Borders:** Soft, thin-line borders (1px solid in `#D5CDBF` or `#9E9380` at 30% opacity) define the edges of containers, keeping the UI light and clean.
- **Backdrop:** Use a soft-focus background blur (32px+) with a semi-transparent warm ivory overlay for modals and menus to keep the underlying structure visible but soft.
## Shapes
The shape language remains structured and architectural. A **Soft (0.25rem)** roundedness is applied to buttons and inputs to prevent them from looking overly sharp while preserving clean, classic lines.
- **Circular Accents:** Use perfect circles for profile imagery, stamps, or the "Uncle Jam" signature seal to create structural variation on the page.
- **Framing:** Use thin-line gold or dark-brown ornamental borders to enclose hero products, echoing the classic framework of the physical jam labels.
## Components
### Buttons
Primary buttons are solid Gold (`#9E7B28`) with white text, utilizing bold Manrope caps. Secondary buttons are "Ghost" style with a thin Gold border and no fill. On hover, buttons display a soft, warm gold outer glow.
### Cards
Cards utilize a pure white background (`#FFFFFF`) with a subtle sandstone border (`#D5CDBF`). Product cards feature crisp photography with soft natural shadows to lift the products off the light canvas.
### Input Fields
Fields are clean and light. Use a `#F5F1EB` warm sand background with a solid bottom border in primary gold. Active fields present a floating label in small-caps Manrope.
### Chips & Tags
Used for quality claims like "100% Natural" or "Hand-Selected." These are styled as small, pill-shaped outlines with a thin gold border, dark text, and no background fill.
### Dividers
Dividers are warm and subtle. Use a 1px solid line in sandstone (`#D5CDBF`) or a soft gradient that fades out at the edges to transition between sections.
