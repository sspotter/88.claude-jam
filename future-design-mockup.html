New ecommerce structure

Separate the shop for customers and the control room for the team.

This mockup turns the project into two focused experiences: a polished storefront for browsing and buying, and a dense admin dashboard for managing products, orders, and inventory without exposing internal tools to shoppers.

View storefront concept View admin concept
Routing idea:
/ -> public shopping /admin -> staff dashboard
Shared brand system

Keep the same logo, warm neutral palette, rounded surfaces, and gold accent so the app feels like one polished product even though the routes are separated.

Customer experience

Make the storefront fast to scan, with a strong search bar, editorial hero, product cards, and simple filtering that feels familiar from the reference.

Admin experience

Give staff a more data-dense workspace with clear metrics, stock alerts, order status, and quick actions that do not overwhelm shoppers.
